Why should you become a freeholder?


  • The cost of extending the lease of a property are similar to purchasing the freehold
  • Owners of freehold properties do not have to pay annual ground rent
  • If you own the freehold, you have a greater freedom on how the property and development are maintained
  • Your property will be worth significantly more than an identical leasehold properties as their lease tenure reduces over time
  • One less hurdle in needing the current freeholderâ€™s approval to resolve matters impacting day to day running of the development

  • We can improve the infrastructure of the site (e.g. Super Fast Broadband Hyper Optics for example currently blocked by freeholder)
  • Negotiate our own property insurance to get a better deal
